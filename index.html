Process

    • 01.

      We set up an appointment to get to know each other, to discuss the project, your expectations and how I work for the success of the project.

    • 02.

      Before we begin, I invite you to the Project Area, a space that will allow us to centralize everything: presentations, returns, files and more. You don't have to get lost in e-mails, it's all in the same place.

    • 03.

      This is the most important step: the strategic call. For a short hour, we exchange information on everything that will allow us to take the right direction for your brand.

      We will talk about the goals, values, competitors, vision and personality of the brand.

    • 04.

      I am now creating two moodboards that will allow us to define more precisely the creative direction to be followed for the entire brand.

      Once validated, I move on to the logo and graphic design part. I'm working on the best solution(s) to meet your needs and I'll present it to you in a few days.

    • 05.

      I propose up to five revisions. Consequently, there is an exchange of views on possible adjustments.

      When this step is complete, I will reveal your visual identity on all communication media

    • 06.

      Once the entire project is validated, you will be able to access all the files you need for the proper use of your branding.

      Your brand is now ready to emerge from the nest with its new branding.
